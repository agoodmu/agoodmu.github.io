On 28th August 2018, I onboarded a plane to Auckland, New Zealand. It is my first trip abroad. I had been working and living in Beijing for 4 years by then, and I didn't know what is the purpose of life since everything is so different from what I was told and taught before I graduated from university.

I felt that i had been living in a same world to which i did not belong.
